School Curriculum

E.S Marie Adelaide is a well-resourced academic environment for student. E.S Marie Adelaide offers a full secondary school program from Senior 1 to Senior 6

Ordinary Level (O Level)

This is a three year program from S1 to S3 and is completed by national examinations. Subjects offered and examined include Mathematics, Physics, Chemistry, Biology, Geography, History, Entrepreneurship, English Language, French, Swahili and Kinyarwanda. We also offer other Christian Based Lesson Such as Religion in order to Develop a good in spirit and knowledge based students for the whole community.

Advanced Level (A Level)

Successful candidates from O’level or equivalent, promote to A’level. This is also a three year program from S4 to S6 leading to ‘A’ Level national examination and a Certificate of Completion from the Rwanda Education Board (REB).

Combinations offered include:

Mathematics Computer Science and Economics (MCE)
Mathematics Economics and Geography (MEG)
